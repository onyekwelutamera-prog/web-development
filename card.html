Indulge in the rich flavour of this juicy, tender beef steak seasoned to perfection loaded with herbs and spices and seared for a rich flavorful crust on the outside while remaining succulent and tender inside. Served with a side of creamy mashed potatoes, sauteed vegetables and a glass of red wine.

Ingredients

Ribeye Steak

4 beef ribeye steaks

Olive Oil

2 tablespoons of Olive oil

Garlic Cloves

2 cloves garlic, minced

Rosemary

1 teaspoon fresh rosemary,chopped

Thyme

1 teaspoon fresh thyme, chopped

Salt and Pepper

Salt and Pepper to taste

Tomatoes

4 seeds of tomatoes

Seasoned Vegetables

5 sticks of Asparagus

potatoes

Cooked and Mashed up potatoes
